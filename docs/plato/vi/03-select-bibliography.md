# *Thư mục tham khảo chọn lọc*

Bobonich, C. *Plato’s Utopia Recast: His Later Ethics and Politics*. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 2002.

Burnyeat, M. “Platonism and Mathematics: A Prelude to Discussion.” Trong cuốn *Mathematics and Metaphysics in Aristotle*, do A. Graeser biên tập, tr. 213–40. Bern: Paul Haupt, 1987.

———. “Plato on Why Mathematics is Good for the Soul.” Trong cuốn *Mathematics and Necessity*, do T. Smiley biên tập, tr. 1–81. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 2000.

———. “Plato.” *Proceedings of the British Academy* 111 (2000): 1–22.

———. “Plato’s Theory of Human Motivation.” *History of Philosophy Quarterly* 1 (1984): 3–21. Được in lại trong cuốn *Reason and Emotion* của ông, tr. 118–37. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1999.

Ferrari, G. *City and Soul in Plato’s Republic*. Sankt Augustin: Academia Verlag, 2003.

Fine, G., chủ biên. *Plato 1: Metaphysics and Epistemology*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1999.

———. *Plato 2: Ethics, Politics, Religion, and the Soul*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1999.

Irwin, T. *Plato’s Ethics*. New York: Oxford University Press, 1995.

Kraut, R., chủ biên. *The Cambridge Companion to Plato*. Cambridge: Cambridge University Press, 1992.

———, chủ biên. *Plato’s Republic: Critical Essays*. Lanham, Md.: Rowman and Littlefield, 1997.

Moravcsik, J., và P. Temko, đồng chủ biên. *Plato on Beauty, Wisdom, and the Arts*. Totowa, N.J.: Rowman and Littlefield, 1982.

Murdoch, I. *The Fire and the Sun: Why Plato Banished the Artists*. Oxford: Oxford University Press, 1977.

Popper, K. *The Open Society and Its Enemies*, tập 1. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1971.

Reeve, C. *Philosopher-Kings: The Argument of Plato’s Republic*. Princeton: Princeton University Press, 1988.

Sachs, D. “A Fallacy in Plato’s Republic.” *Philosophical Review* 72 (1963): 141–58.

Vlastos, G. “The Theory of Social Justice in the Polis in Plato’s Republic.” Trong cuốn *Interpretations of Plato: A Swarthmore Symposium*, do H. North biên tập, tr. 1–40. Leiden: Brill, 1977.

———. “Elenchus and Mathematics: A Turning-point in Plato’s Philosophical Development.” *American Journal of Philology* 109 (1988): 362–96.

Wagner, E., chủ biên. *Essays on Plato’s Psychology*. Lanham, Md.: Lexington Books, 2001.

White, N. *A Companion to Plato’s Republic*. Indianapolis: Hackett Publishing Company, 1979.

———. *Individual and Conflict in Greek Ethics*. Oxford: Clarendon Press, 2002.
